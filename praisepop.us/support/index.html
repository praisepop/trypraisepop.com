Frequently Asked Questions

1Can I write something negative without consequences?
No! We take negativity very seriously, and we have many precautions in place to ensure that the feeds stay positive. If necessary, you will be traced by email and held accountable. If your pops are reported too many times, you will be suspended!
2Is PraisePop available for Android and Windows Phones?
We are hoping to make that a reality in the near future!
3Is PraisePop limited to only high schools?
Not at all! PraisePop can benefit any community with an email domain, and this includes universities, hospitals, and workplaces.
4There’s a pop breaking the rules. Can you take it down?
If it hasn’t already been hidden by other precautions set in place, then we will read it over and take it down if necessary.
5Why are my pops disappearing?
When pops are reported too many times, they are automatically hidden from the feed. If you keep your pops positive, this shouldn’t be a problem. :)
6Can I use emails from gmail, yahoo, hotmail, etc?
No, those are blocked to ensure that all feeds are closed to communities so that using names is safe.

If you have more questions, please contact us.
